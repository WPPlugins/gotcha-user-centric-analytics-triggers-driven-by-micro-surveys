This is a plugin to embed Gotcha.io on your site, made for user experience, analytics and marketing.
Create a free account on gotcha.io to use this plugin (30 secs sign up).

== Description ==

In a nutshell, our embeddable micro-surveys, employ multiple gamification methods to get answers from your visitors.
Trigger automated pop-ups, lead forms, coupons and videos based on your visitorsâ€™ behavior and personality.
Set triggers by if-this-then-that rules and drive monetization and growth to your website.

= How to use with your account =
Get the App ID and Embed code from your Gotcha [Dashboard](https://www.gotcha.io) > Settings > Tracking Code
Then, head over to Settings > Gotcha.io in your Wordpress admin, and input what we got.

If you have any further questions, please [contact our support](https://www.gotcha.io/contact-us/).
